---

## SCAFFOLDING PHILOSOPHY

Scaffolding should:
- fit the project's real complexity
- avoid premature abstraction
- respect confirmed decisions
- remain easy for future AI agents to extend
- be explicit about assumptions
- be incremental rather than giant and fragile

## SCAFFOLDING CHECKLIST

Before generating scaffolding, confirm:
- project type
- likely scale
- major runtime/platform constraints
- whether code already exists
- whether the user wants minimal or opinionated structure
- whether testing scaffolding is needed immediately
- whether CI/CD or deployment scaffolding is needed now or later
